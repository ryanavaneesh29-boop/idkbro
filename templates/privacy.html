Privacy notice

Last updated: 15 May 2026

What we collect

idkbro stores the information needed to run your account: username, email address, display name, bio, password hash, two-step authentication status, posts, media uploads, drafts, follows, blocks, likes, reposts, messages, notifications, and security logs.

Why we use it

We use account data to provide the service, keep accounts secure, prevent abuse, deliver messages and notifications, and let you control your profile and privacy settings.

Cookies

The app uses a strictly necessary session cookie so you can sign in and keep using the service. It does not set analytics or advertising cookies.

How long we keep data

Account content is kept until you delete it or delete your account. Security logs are kept for up to 90 days by default. Rate-limit records are kept briefly to protect the service from abuse.

Your rights

You can download your personal data or delete your account from Settings. Account deletion removes your profile, posts, uploaded media, messages, notifications, sessions, relationship links, and user-specific logs.

Contact

For privacy requests, contact the site operator. If you are in the UK or EU, you may also have rights to access, correct, erase, restrict, object to, or port your personal data, and to complain to your supervisory authority.
