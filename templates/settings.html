How idkbro uses account data and what controls you have.

What we collect

Username, email address, display name, bio, password hash, two-step status, posts, media uploads, drafts, follows, blocks, likes, reposts, messages, notifications, and security logs.

Why we use it

To provide the service, keep accounts secure, prevent abuse, deliver messages and notifications, and let you manage your profile and privacy settings.

Cookies

The app uses a strictly necessary session cookie. It does not set analytics or advertising cookies.

Your rights
